# Strengths

My primary strengths lie in driving performance and organizing structure.

* **Determination:** I am single-minded and goal-directed. I drive projects through with determination.
* **Pragmatism:** I have a business-oriented approach with an emphasis on practical, tangible results. I take decisions quickly and don't get confused by alternatives.
* **Responsibility:** I have a strong sense of responsibility and a strict personal code of conduct. I can be relied upon to apply myself consistently.
* **Planning:** I am efficient, practical, and good at managing risk. I think ahead and build workable plans.
* **Independence:** I am willing to take personal responsibility for events and can hold my own when challenged.
