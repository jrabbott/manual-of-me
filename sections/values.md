# Values

My work preferences are driven by **Power** and **Process**.

* **Influence:** I am motivated by the ability to influence events and people. I want to feel I am in charge and being challenged.
* **Organization:** I place great emphasis on doing things properly and prefer efficient systems].
* **Meritocracy:** I value being materially rewarded for my achievements and having my work officially recognized.
* **Professionalism:** I respect position, experience, and ethical behavior.
* **Results:** I am motivated by producing measurable results rather than abstract concepts.
